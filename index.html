Questions? We've Got Answers

How long does it take to generate an FAQ?

Most FAQs are generated within 3-5 minutes. The process includes scraping your episode page, transcribing audio (if needed), researching relevant keywords, and generating optimized Q&A pairs. You'll receive an email when your FAQ is ready.

Do I need to upload my audio file?

No! Just paste your episode URL. Our system automatically extracts the audio from most major podcast hosts including Buzzsprout, Transistor, Anchor, Podbean, Libsyn, and many others.

What if my episode doesn't have a transcript?

No problem. We automatically transcribe your audio using advanced speech-to-text AI. The transcript is then used to generate contextually accurate FAQs about your episode content.

Can I edit the FAQ after it's generated?

Yes! You receive clean HTML that you can edit in any text editor or directly in your CMS. Many podcasters tweak a few answers or add additional questions before publishing.

What's JSON-LD schema and why does it matter?

JSON-LD is a structured data format that helps search engines understand your content. When you include FAQ schema markup, search engines can display your Q&As directly in search results as rich snippets, increasing visibility and click-through rates.

Will this work with my podcast host?

We support all major podcast hosting platforms including Buzzsprout, Transistor, Anchor/Spotify, Podbean, Libsyn, Captivate, Simplecast, and most others. If your host has a public episode page with an audio player, we can work with it.
